# 💘 Matchmaking Concierge AI

> Premium AI-powered dating concierge for NYC professionals — psychology meets automation

**Stage:** idea · **Type:** ai-agent · **Funding:** bootstrapped
**HQ:** New York, NY

---

## Mission
Occupy the unoccupied middle market: AI-native and psychologist-backed dating concierge at $500-$2,000/month, far cheaper than traditional luxury matchmakers ($10K-$500K+) but with genuine human expertise that pure AI services lack.

## Vision
Become the #1 AI-powered premium dating concierge in major US metros, expanding from NYC to Boston, DC, and SF, combining deep psychology with agentic AI to make meaningful connections accessible to professionals.

## The Problem
Modern dating in NYC has two unsatisfactory extremes: dating apps are cheap but emotionally exhausting and ineffective (71% report burnout), while traditional matchmakers are effective but prohibitively expensive ($10K-$500K+). There is no middle ground offering genuine human expertise with AI efficiency.

## Our Solution
An AI-powered dating concierge that combines six specialized AI agents with a licensed psychologist to manage your entire dating pipeline — from profile optimization to date booking — via WhatsApp/SMS, at 1/10th the cost of traditional matchmakers.
